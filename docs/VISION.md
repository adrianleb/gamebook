## Chosen Direction

**Selected Theme: The Understage (Theme C)**

The Understage offers the richest possibility space for a gamebook:
- Meta-narrative elements that reward reader awareness
- Natural justification for branching paths (stories can be rewritten)
- Strong player agency (you are a "Prompter" who literally prompts action)
- Diverse settings within a unified framework (every genre exists in the Understage)
- Built-in stakes (blurring of fiction and reality)

## Creative Boundaries

### We Will Include
- Characters with genuine interiority (even if that interiority is "written")
- Moral complexity without nihilism
- Humor as coping mechanism and genuine joy
- Consequences that feel fair, even when harsh
- Multiple valid endings that reflect player values

### We Will Avoid
- Cheap meta-humor that breaks investment
- "It was all a story" dismissiveness
- Grimdark for its own sake
- Trivializing the reality/fiction boundary
- Endings that negate player choice

### Gray Areas (Approach with Care)
- Romance with fictional characters (handle the power dynamics thoughtfully)
- Violence against beings who may or may not be "real"
- The ethics of "editing" someone's story without consent
- Player-character knowledge asymmetry (what you know vs. what the Prompter knows)
